< About >

Hi! I’m Gabrielle Sade Barthelmy — a designer, developer, and creative problem solver passionate about building thoughtful digital experiences. I was born in New York City and raised in the vibrant island of Saint Lucia, which shaped both my global perspective and creative drive. I'm currently a junior at Washington University in St. Louis, studying Computer Science with minors in Human-Computer Interaction and the Business of the Arts. With a background that blends technology, design, and business, I love working at the intersection of these disciplines to craft intuitive, user-centered solutions. Whether it's refining an interface, coding up a prototype, or rethinking how users engage with a product, I’m driven by a desire to make tech more accessible, aesthetic, and impactful.
